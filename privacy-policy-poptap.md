# Privacy Policy for PopTap
Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy is issued by **SkyLink Games Ltd** ("we", "us", "our"), and applies to the **PopTap** casual elimination mini game ("the Game") operated by us on the TikTok platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you access and use the Game.

By using the Game, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information in accordance with this policy.

We do NOT collect personal identifiable information without your explicit, voluntary consent. To optimize the Game experience and in-game advertising performance, we may collect the following non-personal information automatically:
- Game progress, level completion data, and tap/elimination gameplay behavior
- In-game advertising views, clicks, and interaction events
- Device type, operating system version, network type, and basic device information
- TikTok OpenID (provided exclusively by TikTok platform, used only for user identification and basic Game service operation)

We use the collected information for the following limited purposes only:
- To maintain, optimize, and improve the Game's performance and user experience

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing
We use third-party services including TikTok Open Platform and its authorized advertising services. These third parties may collect and process your information in accordance with their own independent privacy policies. We strongly recommend you review the official Privacy Policy of TikTok for more details about their data processing practices.

We will NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information to any other third parties without your explicit consent.

## 4. Data Security
**SkyLink Games Ltd** values your trust in providing us your information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable, industry-standard security measures (including data encryption and access control) to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.
