# Privacy Policy for Level Blast Journey
Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy is issued by **SkyLink Games Ltd** ("we", "us", "our"), and applies to the **Level Blast Journey** level-based block elimination mini game ("the Game") operated by us exclusively on the TikTok platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you access and use the Game.

By using the Game, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect
We do NOT collect personal identifiable information without your explicit, voluntary consent. To optimize the Game experience and in-game advertising performance, we may collect the following non-personal information automatically when you use the Game:
- Game progress, level completion data, score records, and block-matching gameplay behavior
- In-game advertising views, clicks, and interaction events
- Basic device information: device type, operating system version, network type, language settings, and application version
- TikTok OpenID (provided exclusively by TikTok platform, used only for user identification and basic Game service operation, no other private data is collected)

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following limited purposes only:
- To maintain, optimize, and improve the Game's performance, level design, and user experience
- To optimize in-game advertising content, delivery, and relevance
- To fix bugs, technical issues, and crashes in the Game
- To comply with TikTok platform requirements and applicable legal obligations
- To respond to your customer service requests and feedback

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing
We use third-party services including TikTok Open Platform and its authorized advertising services. These third parties may collect and process your information in accordance with their own independent privacy policies. We strongly recommend you review the official Privacy Policy of TikTok for more details about their data processing practices.

We will NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal information to any other third parties without your explicit written consent.

## 4. Data Security
**SkyLink Games Ltd** values your trust in providing us your information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable, industry-standard security measures (including HTTPS encrypted transmission, data access control, and regular security audits) to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.

## 5. Your Data Rights
Depending on your region, you may have the following rights under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA):
- The right to access and confirm whether we process your personal information
- The right to correct or update your inaccurate personal information
- The right to request deletion of your personal information (the "right to be forgotten")
- The right to withdraw your consent at any time
- The right to opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the email provided at the end of this policy.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The Game is a general audience casual game, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (per COPPA rules). If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that information. Minors under the age of 18 must use the Game with the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-game notice, and will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
